Personal Profile
by Utkan Gezer

Hello, whoever. My name is Utkan.

I am a man and have been alive for the past 21 years. For all those years, all those days, I have been waking up with the sun, hoping and occasionally trying to get the best out of the life.

On top of everything else, I enjoy bringing people that I truly love on cloud nine the most. For me, it is the heaven on earth to be the bliss of them.

And that unfortunately isn't always possible. I hope more than I do, I find myself killing time more than I live. Decisions; there are no wrong or right decisions and we live our decisions. I occasionally decide on going out and doing one of the following and alikes:

  • Sitting on isolated benches or laying down anywhere uncrowded
  • Viewing grasses and leaves and the sky
  • Cycling and climbing trees
  • Listening to music and daydreaming
  • Creating scenarios on my mind of conversations with people I had met

All alone. The moon and the cats, no wonder why the rest doesn't love me as much.
